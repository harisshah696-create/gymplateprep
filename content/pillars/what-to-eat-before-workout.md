# What Should I Eat Before a Workout? The Perfect Pre-Workout Meal

The right pre-workout meal gives you energy, improves performance, and prevents you from crashing mid-session. The wrong one leaves you sluggish or nauseous.

Here's the formula.

---

## The Ideal Pre-Workout Meal

**Moderate carbs + moderate protein + low fat + low fiber**

| Nutrient | Why | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Carbs (moderate) | Fuels your workout | Oats, rice, banana, bread |
| Protein (moderate) | Primes muscle protein synthesis | Whey, eggs, chicken, yogurt |
| Fat (low) | Slows digestion, can cause nausea | Minimal oil, no heavy sauces |
| Fiber (low) | Can cause bloating | Skip the broccoli before training |

**Timing: 1.5–2 hours before training.**

---

## 6 Sample Pre-Workout Meals

| Meal | Calories | Protein |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Oatmeal + 1 scoop whey + banana | ~450 | 35 g |
| 2 slices toast + peanut butter + banana | ~420 | 16 g |
| White rice + 4 oz chicken + honey | ~500 | 40 g |
| Greek yogurt + granola + berries | ~380 | 25 g |
| 3 eggs + 1 slice toast + avocado | ~440 | 22 g |
| Rice cakes + almond butter + banana | ~350 | 12 g |

These all digest well within the 1.5 hour window.

---

## What NOT to Eat Before a Workout

| Food | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Large fatty meal (burgers, pizza) | Slows digestion, causes sluggishness |
| High-fiber vegetables (broccoli, beans) | Bloating, gas during training |
| Heavy dairy for some people | Can cause stomach issues |
| Spicy foods | Heartburn risk |

---

## What If You Train First Thing in the Morning?

If you train fasted or don't have 1.5 hours to digest, keep it light:

* **Option 1:** 1 banana + coffee (15 min before)
* **Option 2:** 1 scoop whey + water (immediately before)
* **Option 3:** Rice cakes + honey (immediately before)

Something small is better than nothing, but also fine to train fasted if your workout is under 60 minutes.
